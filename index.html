Your Personal Karaoke Coach

Coach-eoke finds the perfect songs for your voice, so you can improve your singing skills and impress your friends at karaoke!

How it Works

  1. It listens to your voice and builds a customized singing profile based on your vocal range.

  2. It searches through a database to find songs that match your voice and skill level.

  3. As you sing more, it tracks your karaoke history, and gives you practice tips.
